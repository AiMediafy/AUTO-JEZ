<h2>Project Glasswing by Anthropic – a new era of multimodal models?</h2><p>Anthropic puts <strong>Project Glasswing</strong> on the table – a fresh initiative that aims to ambitiously combine AI capabilities in processing text, images, and – eventually – video. For the market, this is a significant statement: multimodality is becoming foundational for modern tools, and companies increasingly expect AI to understand not just language, but also the “world as seen.”</p><p><strong>What does Glasswing actually bring?</strong></p><ul><li><strong>Single platform</strong> – a model that interprets text and images in one stream (and eventually video as well). No need to switch tools or APIs.</li><li><strong>Document analysis</strong> – Glasswing is meant to organize the “chaos” within company files: extracting data from reports, contracts, PDFs, photos, diagrams. In practice: no more manual copying and searching for phrases in PDFs.</li><li><strong>Handling complex queries</strong> – users can ask questions like “Identify the risks in this financial document set” and receive an automatic analysis combining report text with table images.</li></ul><h2>Opportunities and challenges – a practitioner’s view</h2><ul><li>Multimodality provides an edge where data is scattered – e.g., in <strong>HR departments</strong> or <strong>compliance</strong> (integration of CVs, certificates, scans with textual info).</li><li>The key here is the <strong>quality of input data</strong> – in practice, if documents are unreadable, poor scans, unusual formats – Glasswing probably won’t “work magic.”</li><li><strong>Workflow integration</strong>: implementation requires building processes – from my perspective, companies often underestimate this phase. AI alone, without process redesign = little value.</li></ul><h2>Real numbers – where to look for ROI?</h2><ul><li>Example: Automatic contract analysis in the legal department – typically this is <strong>15 h/week × 4 people × 80 PLN/h = approx. PLN 19,000 per month</strong> just for “manual digging.” Even 20–30% automation = <strong>PLN 5–6,000 savings</strong> per team.</li><li>Another area: rapid identification of financial risks – instead of waiting 3 days for a paper review, AI does it in a few minutes.</li></ul><h2>Summary</h2><p>From my perspective: Glasswing looks promising, but the key will be <strong>data detection reliability</strong> and <strong>actual integration with business processes</strong>. If Anthropic delivers on quality and repeatability, the market will get a tool for concrete applications – not just a “demo.”</p><p>If you want to discuss practical multimodal AI implementations – write or leave a comment.
