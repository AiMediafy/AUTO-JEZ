<p>Rakuten, the global e-commerce giant, has just shared <a href='https://openai.com/index/rakuten'>impressive results</a> achieved through the use of Codex – the coding agent developed by OpenAI. The main takeaway? They managed to reduce mean time to repair (MTTR) by 50%, automate code reviews (CI/CD), and accelerate full-solution development by weeks. In a world where time is money, such results are truly impressive.</p>  

<h2>What does this mean in practice?</h2>
<p>Codex is an AI model designed to support developers. It can generate code, analyze existing solutions, and improve the development process. Rakuten leveraged it in three key areas:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Reducing MTTR:</strong> Codex identifies and resolves problems far faster than traditional methods. A 50% shorter repair time means not only cost savings but also better customer service levels.</li>
  <li><strong>Automating CI/CD reviews:</strong> Deployment processes require precision, and Codex helps automatically detect code errors, reducing the time spent on manual inspections.</li>
  <li><strong>Accelerating full deployments:</strong> Thanks to AI, teams were able to cut the time required to build and implement solutions from months to weeks.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Business implications</h2>
<p>What does this mean for companies developing software? Primarily, <strong>tangible savings:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>A 50% reduction in MTTR for a DevOps team of 10 people earning an average of 120 PLN/hour translates to approximately 5,000–6,000 PLN weekly savings just in debugging efforts (assuming 10 repair hours per week).</li>
  <li>Automating code reviews can reduce the need for manual testing by about 30%, which can save thousands of PLN per month on large projects.</li>
  <li>Faster deployments mean reduced time-to-market, which results in quicker revenue generation or higher user adoption rates.</li>
</ul>
<p>From my perspective, two aspects are key in such implementations: the quality of the data (i.e., code) and the adaptation of processes to work effectively with AI. Without these, even the best AI agent won't showcase its full potential.</p>

<h2>What's next?</h2>
<p>Codex demonstrates how AI can deliver <strong>real business impact</strong>, but it requires a synergy of technology with people and well-organized processes. If you're considering similar implementations in your company, it's worth starting with a review of processes and data readiness for AI.</p>
