<p><strong>OpenAI has officially achieved compliance with FedRAMP Moderate – for both ChatGPT Enterprise and the API. This is one of the most significant changes for institutions with high security requirements.</strong> FedRAMP is the American federal standard for cloud cybersecurity. Moderate is already a substantial benchmark – meeting over 300 technical and organizational requirements.</p>  <h2>What does this mean in practice?</h2> <ul> <li><strong>U.S. federal agencies</strong> – government offices, agencies, and institutes can test and implement ChatGPT Enterprise and OpenAI’s API without major formal barriers.</li> <li><strong>Banking, insurance, healthcare sectors</strong> – in practice, many large companies follow this standard (even outside the US), requiring it from their cloud vendors and SaaS tools.</li> <li><strong>Technically:</strong> it involves implementing advanced identity management, logging, access control, data encryption at every stage, and audit readiness. This covers, for example, full data segregation, strong backup mechanisms, incident monitoring, etc.</li> </ul> <p>From my perspective: this is a big step, but not a “magic switch” to unlock AI in a corporation or public office.</p> <h2>Opportunities and challenges:</h2> <ul> <li><strong>This is a key signal – without FedRAMP, there’s not even a conversation about wide-scale AI implementation in U.S. public administration.</strong> Similar requirements are also emerging in Europe and the financial sector.</li> <li><strong>Easier AI adoption for large institutions</strong> – formal barriers are disappearing, but the matter of <strong>integration with their own data, processes, and auditability</strong> remains.</li> <li><strong>The IT team</strong> must be able to properly configure the environment, manage access, and respond to incidents. OpenAI’s marketing fluff is no substitute for good internal architecture.</li> <li>AI deployments in large organizations often means: 10–20 full-time staff × 3–6 months of deployment work × PLN 100,000–300,000 to start – if everything “aligns” with the existing infrastructure.</li> </ul> <h2>Conclusion</h2> <p><strong>FedRAMP Moderate compliance really expands the pool of places where ChatGPT Enterprise and the API can be deployed. But security is not just about checklists – the key is adapting processes, access, and IT culture. If you’re considering AI in an institution with heightened requirements, now’s a good time to talk with your compliance and IT teams.</strong></p> <p>Let me know what barriers you see in your organization or industry – I’d be happy to discuss.
